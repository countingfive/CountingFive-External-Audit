## Executive Summary

McKeown Kraai Professional CPAs continues to demonstrate strong search visibility — ranking #1 for four key local search terms and top-5 for all tested keywords except tax preparation. Since the previous audit, the site has added Open Graph tags and testimonials now appear in the crawl, which are meaningful improvements. However, mobile performance remains the firm's most urgent liability: a 7.1-second mobile Largest Contentful Paint means most smartphone visitors abandon before a single word loads. Desktop performance has also declined measurably to a 54/100 score, driven largely by unoptimized image delivery. Schema markup remains absent, limiting how Google surfaces the firm in structured and AI-powered search results. The firm's greatest untapped opportunity is closing the gap between its substantial external credibility — Michael McKeown's Michigan Farm Bureau speaking engagement, the MICPA Small Firm Award, 40+ years of operation, and a team that includes an EA alongside two CPAs — and the generic messaging prospects encounter on the website.

## Mobile Responsiveness
**Grade: D** | **Average Score: 4.2/10**

**Sub-Scores:**

- Mobile Performance: 5/10
- Viewport Configuration: 1/10
- Touch Targets: 4/10
- Text Readability: 4/10
- Lcp Mobile: 1/10
- Cls Mobile: 10/10

Mobile performance is the single most urgent issue on this site. With a Google PageSpeed mobile score of 65 and a Largest Contentful Paint of 7.1 seconds, the majority of smartphone visitors leave before the page finishes loading — and that's more than half of all web traffic. The missing viewport meta tag is a foundational issue: without it, mobile browsers can't properly scale the page, forcing users to pinch and zoom through content. Touch targets are too small for reliable tapping, and font sizes fail mobile readability standards. The only bright spot is a perfect Cumulative Layout Shift score of 0, meaning the page doesn't jump around once it loads. But getting to that load at 7.1 seconds is too high a bar for most visitors to clear.
