## Executive Summary

Korbey Lague PLLP's website significantly undersells a firm with genuine differentiators. A partner holding the rare PFS (Personal Financial Specialist) designation, a Sage Intacct technology partnership, a dedicated nonprofit accounting practice, tiered bookkeeping packages, and three CPAs on a nine-person team — none of these appear as front-facing claims anywhere on the site. The most urgent technical issue is mobile performance: a 12-second mobile LCP means the majority of smartphone visitors abandon the site before a single word loads. No schema markup, no confirmed Google Business Profile, and zero client testimonials complete a picture of a firm with strong substance and weak digital presence. The opportunity here is not to build something new — it is to tell the story that already exists.
