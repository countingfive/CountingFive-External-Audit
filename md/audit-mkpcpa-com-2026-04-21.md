## Executive Summary

McKeown Kraai Professional, CPAs is an established CPA firm serving Southwest Michigan with a strong organic search presence and an impressively modern, lean technology stack. The site ranks #1 for two of its five target keywords and shows excellent desktop performance. However, mobile performance is a significant liability — with a mobile LCP of 6.4 seconds and a missing viewport tag, the site is difficult to use on smartphones, which now account for the majority of web traffic. Content freshness is also a critical gap: the sitemap indicates pages were last updated in September 2020, signaling to search engines that the site may be neglected. The firm's strongest opportunities are activating client testimonials, optimizing Google Business Profile for local pack visibility, and addressing mobile load time — each of which could meaningfully increase inbound inquiries from Southwest Michigan's business community.

## SEO / AIO / GEO Health
**Grade: D+** | **Average Score: 5.12/10**

**Sub-Scores:**

- Title Tags & Meta Descriptions: 2/10
- H1-H6 Heading Structure: 6/10
- Json-Ld / Schema Markup: 7/10
- Open Graph / Social Tags: 2/10
- Sitemap & Robots.Txt: 10/10
- Internal Linking: 6/10
- Keyword Alignment: 7/10
- Llms.Txt / Ai Visibility File: 1/10

SEO fundamentals are a mixed bag. The positives are real: an XML sitemap and robots.txt are both present and properly configured, JSON-LD schema markup was detected by BuiltWith (giving the site a leg up in AI-powered search), and keyword-aligned content exists across service and industry pages. However, two significant gaps stand out. First, no meta descriptions are configured — so search engines auto-generate them from page content, usually with poor results. Second, no Open Graph tags are present, meaning any content shared on social media will appear without a preview image or custom description. The viewport tag is also absent from the crawled HTML, which can confuse mobile browsers. These are all fixable in a single focused SEO sprint. One additional finding: mkpcpa.com has no llms.txt or ai-robots.txt file — an emerging standard that tells AI crawlers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) what content can be cited. Firms that publish this file get a meaningful head start in AI-powered search visibility.

## 💡 Recommendations & Next Steps

### 1. Mobile Performance: LCP 6.4 seconds ( Priority)
**Business Impact:** Over half of search traffic is mobile — a 6.4-second load time means most smartphone visitors leave before seeing the firm's message.


### 2. Stale Content: Last Updated September 2020 ( Priority)
**Business Impact:** Five-plus years without fresh content signals to Google that the site is inactive, quietly eroding rankings for the keywords the firm currently owns.


### 3. Missing Meta Descriptions & Open Graph Tags ( Priority)
**Business Impact:** Without meta descriptions, Google writes its own preview text — usually uninspiring. Missing OG tags mean social media shares show no image or custom headline.


### 4. No Client Testimonials on Website ( Priority)
**Business Impact:** The firm has loyal, long-term clients (8+ year relationships cited in Google reviews), but none of that social proof appears on the website where it could directly convert prospects.


### 5. Absent from Google Local Pack ( Priority)
**Business Impact:** The firm ranks organically but doesn't appear in the Google Maps pack — which sits above all organic results and captures the highest-intent local clicks.
