The opportunity here is not to build something new — it is to tell the story that already exists.

Mobile Responsiveness

F  4.8 ▼

What This Means

Mobile performance scored 55/100 — well below the industry threshold of 70. The mobile Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) of 12.0 seconds is the most critical finding in this audit: Google considers anything above 2.5 seconds a failure, and research shows 53% of mobile visitors abandon a page that takes more than 3 seconds to load. With over 60% of local searches now conducted on smartphones, this single issue is likely costing the firm qualified leads every day. The desktop experience is significantly better at 88/100, suggesting the performance issues are mobile-specific — likely unoptimized images and render-blocking resources that Rootworks CMS is not handling efficiently for mobile delivery.
