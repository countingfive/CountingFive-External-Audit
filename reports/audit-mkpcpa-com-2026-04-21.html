McKeown Kraai Professional, CPAs is an established CPA firm serving Southwest Michigan with a strong organic search presence and an impressively modern, lean technology stack. Technical Site Health

Infrastructure, search optimization, and platform performance findings.

SEO / AIO / GEO Health

D+  5.1 ▼

What This Means

SEO fundamentals are a mixed bag. The positives are real: an XML sitemap and robots.txt are both present and properly configured, JSON-LD schema markup was detected by BuiltWith (giving the site a leg up in AI-powered search), and keyword-aligned content exists across service and industry pages. However, two significant gaps stand out. First, no meta descriptions are configured — so search engines auto-generate them from page content, usually with poor results. Second, no Open Graph tags are present, meaning any content shared on social media will appear without a preview image or custom description. The viewport tag is also absent from the crawled HTML, which can confuse mobile browsers. These are all fixable in a single focused SEO sprint. One additional finding: mkpcpa.com has no llms.txt or ai-robots.txt file — an emerging standard that tells AI crawlers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) what content can be cited. Firms that publish this file get a meaningful head start in AI-powered search visibility.

Mobile Responsiveness

D  4.5 ▼

What This Means

Mobile performance is the most urgent issue on this site. With a Google PageSpeed mobile score of 59/100, the largest contentful paint clocking in at 6.4 seconds, and a missing viewport configuration, a significant portion of visitors — especially those searching on smartphones — are likely abandoning before the page finishes loading. More than half of all web traffic is now mobile-first, and Google uses mobile performance as a primary ranking signal. The good news is that CLS (layout stability) is perfect at 0, meaning the page doesn't jump around. However, the LCP and FCP issues point to render-blocking resources or unoptimized delivery that needs attention.

Site Age, Health & Errors

B-  7.2 ▼

What This Means

Domain history data indicates mkpcpa.com has been registered since approximately 2010 — a 16-year-old domain carries significant authority with search engines, which is a meaningful competitive asset. The site crawl found no broken links, redirect chains are clean (a single www-to-www hop), and all pages appear to be properly crawlable and indexed. The critical issue here is content freshness: the XML sitemap shows the most recent page modification date of September 3, 2020 — over five years ago. Search engines interpret this as a signal that the site is not actively maintained, which gradually erodes rankings for competitive keywords. Even a modest content update schedule — one new article or updated service page per month — would meaningfully improve this signal.
